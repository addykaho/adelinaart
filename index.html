ABOUT ADELINA

Also known as Addy, Adelina is a current college sophomore studying art at Pomona College in Claremont, California. She is currently interning for the Benton Museum of Art. Raised in Lake Tahoe, Nevada, Addy is an outdoors enthusiast whose art often reflects her home.
